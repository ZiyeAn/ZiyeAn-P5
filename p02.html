Project #2 Kirby Generator

Kirby Generator

In this project, I have decided to create a Kirby generator inspired by the game Kirbyâ„¢ and the Forgotten Land. In the game, Kirby can transform into different shapes by sucking in objects. To avoid creating unattractive Kirbys, I have chosen to preset their appearance instead of making their eyes and limbs random. To transform Kirby, simply press and release the mouse.

Design Process

Realizing this idea has proven to be extremely challenging. I researched and applied noise in the transformation process of Kirby. I also attempted to customize the function to make drawing Kirby easier. However, I encountered difficulties when trying to utilize random methods. Although I wrote functions for the transformed status of Kirby, I am unable to incorporate them as assets and use random(). As a result, I sought out tutorials and used random() to generate images that I drew.

Reflection

I learned a lot in this project and I would like to learn more about class and assets to solve the problems that I met. Also, it would be nicer if there were suggestions on how to simplify the code.
