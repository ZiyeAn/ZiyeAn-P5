Project #1 Lost and found

A leather journal Rishikaa lost

I tried to recreate the journal Rishikaa lost in her childhood with code. In order to utilize translate() and rotate() we learned in the class, I drew an open journal which leave space for interaction and movement. I also apply mouseIsPressed and keyIsPressed to enhance the interaction. They can be found at the line 7-10 and line 1946-1949. When pressing the key, the whole journal will rotate, and while mouse is placed on the sheet of the journal and pressed, the sheet can be torn off with the movement of the mouse. The code is pretty long since the AI file I created contains lost of curves.
